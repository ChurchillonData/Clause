# Article 272

The National House of Chiefs shall - under this Constitution or any other law for any matter relating to or affecting chieftaincy; of customary law with a view to evolving, in appropriate cases, a unified system of rules of customary law, and compiling the customary laws and lines of succession applicable to each stool or skin; a view to eliminating those customs and usages that are outmoded and socially harmful; function assigned to the House of Chiefs of a region, as Parliament may refer to it.
