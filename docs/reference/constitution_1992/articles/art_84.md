# Article 84

The functions of the National Security Council include- internal and external security of Ghana; Ghana and the integration of the domestic, foreign and security policies relating to it so as to enable the security services and other departments and agencies of the Government to co-operate more effectively in matters relating to national security; of Ghana in relation to the actual and potential military power in the interest of national security; and policies on matters of common interest to the departments and agencies of the Government concerned with national security.
