# Article 296

Where in this Constitution or in any other law discretionary power is vested in any person or authority - fair and candid; capricious or biased wither by resentment, prejudice or personal dislike and shall be in accordance with due process of law; and officer, there shall be published by constitutional instrument or statutory instrument, regulations that are not inconsistent with the provisions of this Constitution or that other law to govern the exercise of the discretionary power.
