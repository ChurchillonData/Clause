# Article 70

  The President shall, acting in consultation with the Council of State, appoint-
    the Commissioner for Human Rights and Administrative Justice and his Deputies;
    the Auditor-General;
    the District Assemblies Common Fund Administrator;
    the Chairmen and other members of -
    the Public Services Commission; (ii) the Lands Commission; (iii) the governing bodies of public corporations; (iv) a National Council for Higher Education howsoever described; and
    the holders of such other offices as may be prescribed by this Constitution or by any other law not inconsistent with this Constitution.
  The President shall, acting on the advice of the Council of State, appoint the Chairman, Deputy Chairmen, and other members of the Electoral Commission.
