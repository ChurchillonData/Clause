NATIONAL SAMPLE AUTHORITY BILL, 2026

Clause 1—Establishment of the Authority
(1) There is established by this Act an Authority.
(2) The Authority is a body corporate.
(a) The Authority may sue and be sued.
(b) The Authority may acquire property.
(i) The property shall be held for public purposes.

2. Functions of the Authority
(1) The Authority shall regulate services under Act 769.
(2) The Authority shall comply with Article 19(2)(d).
